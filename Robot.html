Human Robot

A humanoid robot is a robot resembling the human body in shape. The design may be for functional purposes, such as interacting with human tools and environments, for experimental purposes, such as the study of bipedal locomotion, or for other purposes. In general, humanoid robots have a torso, a head, two arms, and two legs, though some humanoid robots may replicate only part of the body. Androids are humanoid robots built to aesthetically resemble humans.

The Robots
of CES 2019

Robots, in simple terms, are machines designed to perform tasks autonomously or semi-autonomously. While we often imagine humanoid robots from sci-fi, the reality is that various types of robots are already in use today, and they are evolving rapidly.

The good news is that robots are likely to focus more on handling repetitive or hazardous tasks rather than taking over the world. Now, let's break down what robotics is, understand the role of Artificial Intelligence in it, explore the future of robotics, and see how these technological advancements will impact our daily lives. The bottom line is that robots are here to stay, and they are set to make significant positive changes in various aspects of our lives.
